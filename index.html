Get started

speechDiary is a speech-to-text based web application
that lets you convert your speech into text so that you can maintain your daily diary without any hassle.



Keep a record of your daily adventures and store them forever with you.

Features

This platform allows users to save their daily activities and much more...


speechDiary is made for people of all age groups and with all the featrures that it offers, spending 5 minutes a day on yourself is totally worth it.

speech-to-text


  • Talk about your day and it will be converted into text for you to read later.

  • Save the text and the audio recording along with the current date, time and location.

  • Access the text and recording anytime using the calendar.

Add Images


  • Click pictures and attach them along with your daily page to make your day more memorable.

  • Upload them by dragging or selecting them from your computer.

  • View them on a carousel.

Weather


  • Check the weather forecast at an instance of the current day and next few days.

  • Make your current and future plans accordingly.

  • Access the weather forecast of next few days using the calendar.

to-do List


  • Write down your daily activities and chores so that you never forget to do them.

  • Get the best out of your day by assigning time for each task.

  • Complete the task and cross it off to delete it from the to-do List.

Note Keeping


  • Make notes of the things that are important to you.

  • Add your notes to your wall and access them whenever you want by visiting your personal wall.

  • Delete the notes once you are done with them.

Data Encryption


  • All the data uploaded by you on our databases are encrypted.

  • So don't worry about data theft and misuse of the data uploaded by you.

  • All and any form of data uploaded by you is encrypted including your to-do list, images, audio, text diary and notes.
